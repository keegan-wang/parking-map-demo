How it works

1

Tell it where you're going. Type any NYC address or just long-press the map to drop a pin.

2

Pick when, and for how long. Rules change by the hour — Curbs only recommends spots that stay legal for your entire stay.

3

Park. Take a free spot, a meter with the price already totaled, or book a garage at a guaranteed rate.

Always verify posted signs. Curbs is built from official NYC data and we show you the verbatim signs for every block — but signs change and temporary restrictions happen. The sign on the street is the law.

Built on the city's own data

Curbs runs on official NYC Open Data — the DOT sign database, ParkNYC meter rates, licensed garage records, and the street-cleaning suspension calendar — rebuilt nightly with quality checks. When street cleaning is suspended, Curbs knows the same morning.
