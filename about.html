About NYC Parking

Always verify the posted signs. This app is informational only. It is built from New York City's official open datasets, but signs change, datasets lag, temporary restrictions (construction, events, emergencies) may not appear, and our automated interpretation of sign text can contain errors. The signs on the street are the law — not this app. We are not responsible for parking tickets, towing, or other consequences of relying on this information.

What this is

Enter a destination, arrival time, and how long you're staying; the app evaluates every parking sign near your destination and recommends where to park — ranked by legality for your full time window, estimated cost, and walking distance. Tap any curb to see its rules and the verbatim text of the actual posted signs, so you can always double-check our interpretation.

Data sources

  • Street parking rules: NYC DOT Parking Regulation Locations and Signs (updated daily by DOT)
  • Meter rates: NYC DOT ParkNYC Block Faces
  • Street geometry: NYC Centerline (CSCL)
  • Garages & lots: NYC DCWP Issued Licenses
  • Street-cleaning suspensions: NYC DOT Alternate Side Parking calendar (scheduled suspensions; same-day emergency suspensions may not be reflected)
  • Geocoding: NYC GeoSearch (NYC Dept. of City Planning)
  • Base map: OpenFreeMap · © OpenStreetMap contributors

Cost estimates

Street costs use the city's published meter rates for your stay duration. Garage prices are not published by the city; garage results link to booking services. Booking links may be affiliate links that pay us a commission at no extra cost to you.

Privacy

No account, no tracking pixels, no sale of personal data. Addresses you search are sent to NYC's GeoSearch service to find coordinates and are not stored by us. If analytics are enabled they are aggregate and cookie-free.
